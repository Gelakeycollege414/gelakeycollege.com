About Our College

Gelakey College Was Established in 1974 and is Located in The Assam-Nagaland Border Area. Gelakey College is In the Only Institution Of Higher Learning in the tea-growing area. The Only Stream of College Is the Arts Stream and The Number Of students is Around 600 students. The College has Honours in Assamese, Political Science,Sociology,History,Education And Economics. The College has the Necessary Teachers in Every department. Free Education is available to students below the poverty line and all facilities are provided to support the physical and mental development of the students. Our goal to move the region towards light.
